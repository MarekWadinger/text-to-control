A farmer is growing two types of vegetables: Tomatoes and Cucumbers.

Available resources:

- Land: 1000 m²
- Water: 3000 liters

Profit per plant:

- Tomatoes: 4 €
- Cucumbers: 3 €

Constraints:

- Each Tomato plant requires 2 m² of land and 5 liters of water.
- Each Cucumber plant requires 1 m² of land and 3 liters of water.

Objective:

- Maximize total profit from selling the vegetables
